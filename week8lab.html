#2. Identify and display the names of the top 10 highest-scoring batters with their total runs scored.
batter_total = deliveries.groupby('batter')['batsman_runs'].sum()
batter_total = batter_total.sort_values(ascending=False)
sns.barplot(x=batter_total[:10],y=batter_total.index[:10],orient='h')
plt.title('Top 10 highest scoring batters')
plt.show()

Grouped by batter and summed up batsman_runs to get the total runs per batter, on sorting in descending order and picking the top 5, I get the 5 best batsmen.

# 5. Tabulate and visualize the batsmen (top 5) involved in the most run-outs.
